Neil Kodner

I'm a hobbyist photographer based in San Jose, California with a passion for capturing motion, light, and detail across a handful of subjects I can't stop pointing a camera at.

Aviation pulled me in first — there's something about a machine that large defying gravity with that much elegance. Hockey came next, a sport tailor-made for photography: fast, physical, and lit like a stage. Birds keep me patient and humble, and the occasional travel assignment gives everything else context.

This site is a living archive of what I've shot. No prints for sale, no client work — just the photos.
